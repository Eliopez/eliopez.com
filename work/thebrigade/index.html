The Brigade
Agency: Van Holtz Co Full Stack Developer: Eric Van Holtz

The Brigade is a digital agency in PDX.

The website was designed with sharp lines, angles and extended backgrounds. Its goal was for a fast, precise, and moody feel.

The Brigade's main priority for the new website was having it powered by a CMS so that anyone on the team can make updates.

A big development challenge was allowing for backgrounds to overflow the page content, angled clip paths, animated lines, images, and text all intersecting at just the right spots.

All of this while still being responsive, and looking great on any device. The design encourages the viewer to scroll down the page, and provides a nice flow between content.
